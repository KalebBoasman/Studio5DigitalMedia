Mediated Expression Assignment 1

Orb Farm Simulation

Review Questions

  1. What genre of creative software do you believe it belongs to?
    I believe the genre of this software would be a sandbox.
  2. List three other examples of software in this genre
    • Powder tool
    • People Playground
    • Universe Sandbox
  3. List the program's interactive features/mechanics (one sentence max explanation per feature/mechanic)
    • Material select panel lets you pick what to spawn into the sandbox.
    • Clicking within the main frame will spawn whatever you currently have selected.
    • The creatures and materials spawned into the world will interact with each other based on their types.
    • A daylight cycle changes the behaviour of some creatures.
    • The fast forward button increasest the speed at which interactions occur and at which the day/night cycle progresses.
    • A gauge on the main panel measures the CO2 and O2 content of the simulation.
  4. What features/mechanics indicate its genre by aligning with its conventions?
    The ability to infinitely spawn in whatever material you choose and the fact that they all interact independently. There is also no end goal or score.
  5. What features/mechanics are novel for its genre?
    The ability to fast forward through time and select from a large variety of materials/creatures/items.
  6. What features/mechanics common to its genre are missing or might be added?
    The ability to pause the simulation and the ability to export/import simulation states.
  7. What context, community or niche is it primarily aimed at and what indicates this?
    It is primarily aimed at marine enthusiasts as the software is built around balancing the CO2 and O2 levels of a marine environment.
  8. What did you find annoying and/or boring about interacting with it?
    There was a fairly small amount of materials/creatures to select from and generally a problem had a direct solution which didnt allow for much play.
  9. What did you find enjoyable about interacting with it?
    The visual style is gorgeous, the CO2 and O2 gauge adds feedback to the experience on whether you're doing good and the shifting day/night cycle that changed behaviours stopped you from striking a perfect balance and stagnating.
